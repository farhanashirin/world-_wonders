World Wonders

Taj mahal

The Taj Mahal is a stunning white marble mausoleum located in Agra, India. It was commissioned in 1632 by the Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan to house the tomb of his favorite wife, Mumtaz Mahal. Here are some key points about the Taj Mahal

effel Tower

It was constructed as the entrance arch to the 1889 World's Fair (Exposition Universelle), which was held to commemorate the 100th anniversary of the French Revolution

Statue of liberty

The statue was designed by French sculptor Frédéric Auguste Bartholdi and its metal framework was built by Gustave Eiffel, the engineer behind the Eiffel Tower

colosseum

Construction of the Colosseum began under Emperor Vespasian around AD 70-72 and was completed in AD 80 under his successor and heir, Titus. Further modifications were made during the reign of Domitian.
